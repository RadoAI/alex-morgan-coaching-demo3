My Story

I've been where you are.

I spent over a decade in corporate leadership — London, Singapore, and eventually Dubai. I ran teams, hit targets, climbed the ladder. And somewhere in the middle of all of that, I realised I was chasing someone else's version of success.

The decision to leave corporate and build my own practice was the hardest and best thing I've ever done. I know exactly what it feels like to be stuck at a crossroads, to second-guess a bold move, to wonder whether you're making the right bets with your one career.

"The goal isn't a better version of the life you have. It's the life you actually want."

I've now worked with over 200 professionals across three continents — entrepreneurs, senior executives, expats building something new in the Gulf. The thread that connects every successful client is the same: they were willing to be honest about where they were stuck, and committed to doing the actual work.

If that sounds like you, I'd love to talk.

What I Believe

The principles behind my coaching

Clarity before strategy

Most people don't have a strategy problem. They have a clarity problem. Until you're honest about what you actually want, no strategy will stick.

Action over insight

Insight without action is just expensive therapy. Every session ends with a concrete commitment. The work happens between sessions, not during them.

Honesty over comfort

I'll tell you what I actually think, not what you want to hear. That's the only way this works. If you want someone to validate your current thinking, I'm not the right coach.

FAQ

Questions before you book?

How do online sessions work? ▾
All online sessions run via Zoom or Google Meet — your choice. You'll receive a calendar invite with the link immediately after booking. Sessions are conducted in a private, distraction-free setting. I recommend you do the same for maximum focus.
What if I need to reschedule? ▾
Life happens. You can reschedule any session with at least 24 hours notice at no cost. Last-minute cancellations (under 24 hours) are counted as a used session for the 3-Month Program, but I apply this policy with common sense — genuine emergencies are treated differently.
Do you offer refunds? ▾
Single Sessions are refundable if cancelled more than 48 hours before. For the 3-Month Program, I offer a pro-rated refund for any unused sessions if you need to exit early.
Is coaching confidential? ▾
Absolutely. Everything discussed in sessions is strictly confidential. I adhere to the ICF Code of Ethics, which includes strong confidentiality standards.
Can we meet in person in Dubai? ▾
Yes. For clients in the UAE, I'm happy to meet in person at a location in Dubai Marina or DIFC. In-person sessions are available for the Single Session and 3-Month Program.
